Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines designed to think, learn, and problem-solve like humans. It encompasses a wide range of technologies, from machine learning, where systems improve through experience, to natural language processing, which enables computers to understand and generate human language. AI is used across various industries, enhancing efficiency, automating tasks, and offering insights through data analysis. While AI has the potential to revolutionize fields like healthcare, finance, and transportation, it also raises ethical concerns regarding job displacement, privacy, and the control of advanced technologies.
